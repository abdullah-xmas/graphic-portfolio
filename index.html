FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do we get started?

Send your video title, topic, and any style reference you like. I’ll use that to create a thumbnail that fits your content and audience.

How fast can you deliver a thumbnail?

Most thumbnails are delivered within 12 to 24 hours. If your upload is urgent, faster delivery may also be possible.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes. Revisions are included so the final thumbnail feels right for your video, style, and channel.

What do you need from me to design the thumbnail?

I usually need the video title, topic, your idea if you have one, and any useful reference or inspiration image.

Can you design thumbnails for my niche?

Yes. I can design for gaming, AI, tech, finance, reaction, commentary, business, and other content styles.

What if I do not like the first version?

No problem. You can request revisions, and I’ll adjust the design so it matches your content better.
