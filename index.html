Questions

Simple answers about briefing, delivery, revisions, pricing, and safe ordering.

How do we get started?

Send your video title, topic, target audience, deadline, and any reference style you like. I’ll use that to plan a clear thumbnail direction before designing.

How fast can you deliver a thumbnail?

Delivery depends on the brief and order package. For urgent uploads, message first so the timeline can be confirmed clearly before ordering.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes. Revisions are included based on the order scope. Clear feedback helps refine the design faster.

Yes. I can design thumbnails for gaming, AI, tech, finance, commentary, business, education, and other creator niches. Each design is matched to the topic and audience.

Send clear feedback and I’ll revise the design based on the order scope, so the final thumbnail fits your video better.

You can start through Fiverr for secure payment and delivery. You can also message first on WhatsApp or email to confirm the direction, price, and deadline.
