FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do we get started?

Send your video title, topic, and any style reference you want followed. That gives me a clear direction for the design.

Yes. Revisions are included so the final thumbnail feels right for your content and your audience.

I usually need the video title, topic, your main idea if you have one, and any useful reference image or inspiration.

Yes. I can design for gaming, AI, tech, finance, commentary, reactions, business, and other YouTube content styles.

No problem. You can request changes and I will refine the design so it matches your video better.
